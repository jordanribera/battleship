How to play:

The object of Battleship is to sink all of your opponentâ€™s ships.

Each player will begin by entering their name.

The players will then take turns shooting and trying to destroy the ships in their opponent's fleet.

During a turn, the player clicks may click a square on the battlefield grid to fire only once.

The square will turn red if the shot hits a ship and white if it hits empty sea.

When all of the squares of a ship have been hit, the ship is sunk.

When a player sinks all of the opponent's ships, the game is won.
